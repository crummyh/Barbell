What is Barbell?

Barbell is an open platform for AI in FRC. Barbell allows teams to upload raw match images and download polished datasets for development.

Project Goals

  • Raise the limits of AI in FRC
  • Lower the requierments to inovate

Why Barbell exists

After the 2025 FRC season, I started thinking about the role of AI object detection in competition. I found that most teams where doing basic gamepiece detection, but only a select few were doing more. I also needed somthing to do over the summer. So, I started development on Barbell. It ended up taking longer then I thought, but I am proud of how it has turned out.

Who is Barbell for?

Barbell is for anyone! It is primarly for FRC teams, but you can also use it as an individual, whether as a resercher, developer, or if you're just curious. Just create an account and get started!
